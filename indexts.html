Metaphor

Metaphor is a mapping from one domain to another. For example,moo is to coe and beep is to car.These mappings get so buried in our language that we don't even notice their effects.

For example, take a language to describe a debate:

"He shot down her ideas."
"She defended her position"
"She overwhilmed over his arguments."
"He had to retreat from his position."

There are all debate-as war as metaphor.It's a zero sum game.Someone wins someone loses.The idea that both people can learn from each other just doesn't come up.
